Scoring Factors Commands
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Scoring factors are used to decide which weapon system/target pairing should be used. Priority is given to the pairing with the higher scoring factor. The individual scoring factors are effectively the weight placed on that parameter during the decision process.

.. command:: weapon_type <integer>

   The scoring factor applied to weapon type in weapon score calculation. An integer value in the range 0 to 10.

   **Default:** 1

.. command:: weapon_subtype <integer>

   The scoring factor applied to weapon subtype in weapon score calculation. An integer value in the range 0 to 10.

   **Default:** 1

.. command:: intercept_range <integer>

   The scoring factor applied to weapon intercept range in weapon score calculation. An integer value in the range 0 to 10.

   **Default:** 1

.. command:: intercept_time <integer>

   The scoring factor applied to weapon intercept time in weapon score calculation. An integer value in the range 0 to 10.

   **Default:** 1

.. command:: pk <integer>

   The scoring factor applied to weapon probability of kill in weapon score calculation. An integer value in the range 0 to 10.

   **Default:** 1

.. command:: workload <integer>

   The scoring factor applied to weapon work load in weapon score calculation. An integer value in the range 0 to 10.

   **Default:** 1
